A clean, ad-free Sudoku designed for a relaxing experience — no timers, no pressure, just the puzzle.

DIFFICULTY LEVELS
• Easy — great for a calm session or if you're new to Sudoku
• Medium — a satisfying challenge for regular players
• Hard — for when you want to focus deeply

NOTES MODE
Tap the Notes button to switch into notes mode. Each cell shows a compact 3×3 mini-grid so your pencil marks are always neatly positioned — digit 1 top-left, digit 9 bottom-right.
When you place a digit, conflicting notes are removed automatically from the same row, column, and box.

HINTS & SOLVER
Stuck? Use the Hint button to reveal one correct digit. Or open the built-in Solver to enter any puzzle and find its solution instantly.

SMART HIGHLIGHTING
• Tap a cell to highlight all cells in the same row and column
• Tap a digit to highlight all matching digits on the board
• Conflicting digits are highlighted in red

UNDO & CLEAR
Undo any number of moves, all the way back to the start of the game. Clear a cell with a single tap.

SAVE & CONTINUE
Your game is saved automatically after every move. Come back later and continue exactly where you left off.

THEMES
Choose from four color themes — Orange, Green, Blue, and Purple — and switch between light and dark mode using your system setting.

STATISTICS
Keep track of how many puzzles you've completed at each difficulty level.
