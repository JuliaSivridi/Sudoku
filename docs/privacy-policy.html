Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Sudoku app ("the App") handles information. The App was created as a personal project and is provided as-is.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no analytics, no crash reporting services, and no advertising SDKs included.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences:

  • Your current game state (board, difficulty, notes)
  • The number of completed puzzles per difficulty level
  • Your selected color theme

This data never leaves your device and is only used to provide the save/continue and statistics features within the App.

Permissions

The App does not request any special Android permissions (no camera, microphone, location, contacts, or internet access).

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions, you can open an issue on the GitHub repository.
